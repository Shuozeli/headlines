Construction crews broke ground today on a new highway bridge spanning
River Q. The project is funded jointly by the state and federal
governments and is expected to be complete in three years.

Traffic on the existing two-lane crossing will be partially diverted
during construction. The transportation authority has published a
detour map and a project timeline.
