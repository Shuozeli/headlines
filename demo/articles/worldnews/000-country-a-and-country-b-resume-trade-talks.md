Negotiators from Country A and Country B met for a third round of trade
talks today, focused on harmonizing tariffs on agricultural products.

The talks, held in a neutral capital, are expected to continue for two
more days. Officials from both delegations declined to predict an outcome
but described the tone as "constructive."

A joint communique is expected at the conclusion of the meetings.
