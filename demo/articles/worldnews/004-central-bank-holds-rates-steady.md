The Country A central bank announced today that it will hold its
benchmark interest rate steady at the current level, citing mixed
inflation signals and steady employment data.

The accompanying statement noted that further rate decisions will
depend on incoming data, particularly the next two inflation prints.

Markets reacted with a modest move in long-end yields.
