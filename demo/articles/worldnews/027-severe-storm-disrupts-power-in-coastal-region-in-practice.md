A severe storm system passed through the coastal region late yesterday,
leaving approximately 80,000 households without power at peak.

No injuries have been reported. Schools in two counties announced
they will remain closed today.


Utility crews from neighboring service areas have been dispatched to
assist with restoration. The utility expects to reach 90 percent of
affected customers by tomorrow evening.
